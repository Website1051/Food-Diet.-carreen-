Healthy Eating Guidelines

Follow these key dietary principles to maintain a balanced and nutritious diet.

1. Eat a Variety of Foods

Include fruits, vegetables, whole grains, and lean proteins in your meals.

2. Maintain a Balanced Diet

Ensure a proper mix of carbohydrates, proteins, and healthy fats.

3. Stay Hydrated

Drink at least 8 glasses of water daily to support overall health.

4. Limit Sugar and Salt Intake

Reduce consumption of added sugars and processed foods high in salt.

5. Practice Portion Control

Eat moderate portions to prevent overeating and maintain a healthy weight.

6. Avoid Ultra-Processed Foods

Opt for natural and whole foods instead of processed snacks and fast food.
